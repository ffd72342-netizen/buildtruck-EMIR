Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions before you reach out

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple website might take 4-6 weeks, while complex applications can take 3-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! We work with clients worldwide and have successfully delivered projects across different time zones. Our communication tools ensure seamless collaboration regardless of location.

What if I need changes after launch?

We include a certain number of revisions in our packages. For ongoing changes, we offer maintenance plans or can discuss additional work on a case-by-case basis.

How do you handle project communication?

We use a combination of email, project management tools, and video calls. You'll have direct access to your project manager and regular progress updates throughout the project.
